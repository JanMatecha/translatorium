Price

Price

The price provides indicative prices for translations, interpreting and proofreading. The final price is determined individually according to the agreement with the customer with regard to the number of standard pages and expertise of the order so that a high quality within specified deadline could be kept. I offer discounts in case of long-term cooperation and bulk orders.

The translations can be charged based on source word count and number of repetitions, which pays primarily for translations with often repeated terms or with repeated translations of similar subject matter.

Terms and conditions

  • After the translation / interpreting, an invoice is issued with payment terms 14-21 days. For multiple pages translations (over 25 standard pages), a deposit is always required after delivery of the first half of the translation. The deposit is determined individually.
  • Payment by bank transfer / cash after delivery.
  • I am not subject to VAT.
