Proofreading

I provide linguistic revisions of texts in Czech in printed and electronic form. These include a check of misspelling and grammar.

  • linguistic revisions for individual customers (seminar, bachelor's and master's theses, dissertation and habilitation theses, papers, etc.)
  • linguistic revisions for companies and publishers (proofreading of presentations, pre-press proofreading of books or dissertation theses)

Proofreading of electronic documents is performed in ordinary text editors such as MS Office and Open office. The changes are marked in color or in the form of revisions, according to customer.

I also provide proofreading of texts in English by native speaker.
